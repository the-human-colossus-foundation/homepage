The Human Colossus Foundation

Synergy for the future

The convergence of technology and data is propelling us into an era of artificial intelligence. Instant access to information continues to broaden the scope of our fascination with final frontier exploration and the rich fauna and flora diversity here on Earth. It also facilitates improved personal healthcare and the development of life-changing cures for rare, chronic and fatal diseases.

“Data” is the fuel behind these quality of life advancements, bringing a deeper level of insight, knowledge and value to civil society. In conjunction with these insights, we are witnessing the dawn of an emerging decentralized data economy where prosperity, freedom, fairness and education can be enjoyed by all.

Welcome to the Human Colossus Foundation, a technological home for collaborative projects for the betterment of humanity and the planet. Our vision is to facilitate a new paradigm in web interaction where innovative human-centric business models can be tested and developed by leveraging user-centric services, data aggregation, self-sovereign identity and distributed trust frameworks.

We are a non-profit foundation based in Geneva, Switzerland.

Decentralized Data Economy

Decentralized Data Economy (DDE) is a new ecosystem that has emerged out of data usage and privacy expectations in the digital world. The main goal of the Human Colossus Foundation is to facilitate the creation and subsequent development of DDE by helping communities, businesses and individuals to integrate with (and participate in) this new ecosystem on equal terms.

We have identified the following key components for the integral development of DDE:

  • Overal Capture Architecture (OCA)
  • Trusted Digital Assistant (TDA)
  • Meaningful Service Providers (MSP)
  • Purpose based consent (PBC)
  • Purpose based Services (PBS)
